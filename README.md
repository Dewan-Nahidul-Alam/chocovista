# ChocoVista Commercial Performance & Business Intelligence Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

The annual commercial sales performance analysis of **ChocoVista Retail Ltd**, with the objective of building an executive-level business monitor to support critical decision-making and forecasting.

A centralized Business Intelligence solution was developed to evaluate revenue performance, profitability, discount effectiveness, and operational efficiency across multiple countries, product categories, and sales channels. Also included scenario analysis and strategic recommendations to improve commercial performance for the following financial year.

## 📊 Responsibilities

* Cleaned, transformed, and modeled approximately **200K transaction records** covering two financial years.
* Developed an interactive dashboard to monitor revenue, gross margin, discount performance, and target attainment.
* Performed country, product category, and sales channel performance analysis.
* Built power query measures to calculate commercial KPIs, year-over-year growth, and performance against business targets.
* Conducted scenario analysis using Goal Seek techniques to determine sustainable promotional discount thresholds.
* Produced executive-level insights and strategic recommendations for commercial planning.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights Generated

### 1. Country Performance Analysis

* Identified **Canada** and the **UK** as the highest revenue-generating markets.
* Determined **Germany** as the lowest-performing market, highlighting the need for a focused market review and localized commercial strategy.

---

### 2. Product Portfolio Performance

* **Praline** and **White Chocolate** emerged as the strongest revenue contributors.
* Identified the **Milk Chocolate** category as the weakest performer, suggesting opportunities for product rationalization and portfolio optimization.

---

### 3. Store Channel Performance

* Compared profitability across **Retail, Mall, Airport, and Online** channels.
* Determined that **Retail** and **Online** generated the strongest gross margins, while **Airport** stores represented the weakest-performing channel.

---

### 4. Discount Effectiveness Analysis

* Quantified the inverse relationship between promotional discounting and gross margin.
* Demonstrated that higher discount levels consistently reduced profitability across all markets and store formats.

---

### 5. Scenario Planning

* Built a commercial scenario model to determine the **maximum sustainable average discount rate** before gross margin fell below the company's profitability threshold.
* Established an evidence-based ceiling of **12.86%** for promotional discounting to support future pricing decisions.

---

## 🏆 Project Outcome

The analysis enables management to:

* Establish a centralized "single source of truth" for commercial performance reporting.
* Prioritize investment in high-performing markets and sales channels.
* Optimize product portfolio strategy based on revenue contribution and profitability.
* Develop a discount governance framework.
* Improve future promotional planning through scenario-based decision making.
* Support FY2025 commercial planning with recommendations based on the comprehensive FY2023-24 performance.
